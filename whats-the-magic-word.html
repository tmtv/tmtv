What's The Magic Word?

by Mr. Joe

With characters from the "The Extra Ordinary Orville" camp, Begonia is asked by Orville, Oggie, Petunia and Rocky the french bulldog for food they love. An enlightening and fun way to teach the "Magic Word" of "Please" when asking for something. An easy to read, funny book for any Mighty Beginner. Get your kids a copy today!
